About

Crazy Raccoon entered Overwatch in April 2024 by signing the free-agent roster “WAC.” The lineup features LIP & HeeSang (DPS), JunBin & MAX (Tank), and the support duo CH0R0NG and Shu. They quickly claimed high-profile titles and continued winning through 2025. Crazy Raccoon went undefeated in maps through the OWCS Asia Stage 2 Korea Regular Season, emerging as the undisputed first seed for the Playoffs. Many analysts considered them nearly unbeatable, especially after their rivals Team Falcons took several losses in the season.
